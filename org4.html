    About the Organization

    La Salle Computer Society (LSCS) is DLSU's largest and pioneering technological organization and the home organization of the College of Computer Studies. LSCS is committed to help students learn more about tech through interactive workshops, make new friends with recreational events, develop their leadership skills through the esteemed Junior Officer Training Program, secure a job through various career events, and more. Now in its 40th year of service, LSCS continues to ignite, innovate, and inspire.

    Vision

    We envision the La Salle Computer Society to be an organization that, through quality assistance and activities, will mold its members academically, socially and spiritually in order for them to become competent Lasallian students and well-rounded individuals. We also strive to continuously serve as the pioneering student organization of the De La Salle University Manila that strongly symbolizes the expertise of the College of Computer Studies (CCS).

    Mission

    The La Salle Computer Society shall serve as a venue for growth and development of its members through a three-step course of:

    Purpose
    To know and understand the reason behind every act, decision, and endeavor pursued.

    Process
    To organize and oversee the entire procedure of every project and make sure that each goes through every necessary step towards the purpose.

    Excellence
    To accomplish our goals in the best way possible and in accordance with the ideals of the organization and of De La Salle University Manila.
