Frequently Asked Questions

OrgSpace is designed to help students easily discover and stay updated with DLSU Organizations through a centralized platform. Below are some commonly asked questions regarding how the system works.

What is OrgSpace?
OrgSpace is a hub where DLSU students can discover student organizations, learn what they do, and access updates in one place.
Who can post announcements or updates?
Only verified organizations can publish official posts to ensure the information is accurate and trusted.
Who can use OrgSpace?
All DLSU students can browse organization profiles. Organizations can manage their org profiles and publish posts.
How do I reach organizations?
Each organization profile includes contact details and links to their official media pages or email addresses.
I have a question. Who can I contact?
You can message the OrgSpace team via the Contact Us page, or email the organization directly using the details on their page.
